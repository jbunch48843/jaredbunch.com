New Project

Fair Play Engine

Making sure every kid gets a fair turn on the field by taking the math out of youth sports rotations.

The Problem

Coaches have to juggle a lot of rules while the game moves fast. It is easy to lose track of who has played and who has not.

My Role

I am the designer and developer on this one. I built the logic and designed the high-contrast interface for the field.

The Goal

The app handles the math so the coach can focus on the kids. It makes sure every player gets exactly the right amount of time.

The Problem with Paper

Managing a roster on paper is tough when you have rules about minimum rest and maximum innings. I wanted to turn those invisible rules into something simple that works in real time.

Designed for the Sideline

[Visual: Mobile interface showing rotation grid]

I designed specific "Field Mode" interactions that are easy to use in direct sunlight and high stress environments. It supports the coach without distracting from the game.
